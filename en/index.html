Welcome to VolgaCTF, an annual open competition in the field of cybersecurity

WHAT IS

VOLGA CTF?

It is an international contest that gathers more than 1000 teams from all around the globe.

Competition finals are held in Samara city and are hosted by Samara University in cooperation with IT and Communications Department of Samara Region

VOLGA CTF

IS:

The meeting place for active and committed young specialists in the field of cyber security.
The forum site where leading specialists both from Russia and from abroad share their experience with the new generation of cyber security professionals.
VolgaCTF is friendship!
